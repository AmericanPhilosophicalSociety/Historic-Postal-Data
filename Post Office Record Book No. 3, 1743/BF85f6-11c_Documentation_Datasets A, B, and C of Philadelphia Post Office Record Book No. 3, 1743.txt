Data description:
Dataset A contains the bulk of the data derived from this book. Each row has been assigned a numerical ID to allow sorting while preserving the data's context.

Dataset B consists of totals by monetary amount in Pounds, Shillings, Pence, which were written at the bottom of each page of the volume. This data was separated from Dataset A during the transcription process.

Assumptions:
Dittos (Do.) are silently corrected and assumed to refer to the information in the above row. 

Normalization and Data Standards:
Names of account holders are split into title, first name, last name. Accounts for companies are entered under last name. Date is separated into year, month, date. Amount due monetary denominations are split into Pounds, Shilling, Pence.
